Support

Got Questions?

How do I pay? ▼
We accept bKash, Nagad, and Cash on Delivery (Dhaka only). After placing your order, send the amount to our number and enter the Transaction ID. Your order is confirmed once we verify payment.
How long does delivery take? ▼
Orders are dispatched within 24 hours. Delivery within Dhaka takes 1–2 working days. Outside Dhaka: 3–5 working days. You'll receive a WhatsApp update when your order ships.
What sizes do you offer? ▼
We offer S, M, L, XL, and XXL. All our tees are oversized cut — if you prefer a regular fit, size down one. Check the Size Guide in any product modal for exact measurements.
Can I return or exchange? ▼
We accept exchanges within 7 days for manufacturing defects or wrong items. Custom orders and used tees cannot be returned. Contact us on WhatsApp with your order ID and photos.
Do you do custom/bulk orders? ▼
Yes! We handle custom designs from 1 piece with a 5–7 day turnaround. For bulk orders (10+ pieces), message us on WhatsApp for a special rate.
How do I care for my tee? ▼
Machine wash cold (30°C max), inside out. Do not tumble dry or bleach. Hang dry for best results. This preserves the print and fabric for years.
Still need help?

We respond within minutes on WhatsApp. You can also reach us on Facebook or by email — whatever works for you.
