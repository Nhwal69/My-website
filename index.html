Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

How long does delivery take? ▼
Dhaka Metro: next business day. Rest of Bangladesh: 2-4 business days. You will receive a tracking number once your order ships.
What sizes are available? ▼
We carry S, M, L, XL, and XXL. Our tees are oversized fit — if you prefer regular fit, size down one.
How do I pay? ▼
We accept bKash, Nagad, Rocket, and Cash on Delivery. For COD, a small advance may be required for orders above ৳2000.
Will I get an email confirmation? ▼
Yes! You will receive an automatic email confirmation the moment you place your order. The shop owner is also instantly notified by email with your full order details.
Can I return or exchange? ▼
Yes! 7-day exchange policy. Defect or wrong item gets a free replacement. Size exchange accepted within 7 days (unused, unwashed).
How do I place a custom order? ▼
Use the Custom Order button — fill the form and we will email you a mockup within 24 hours. Minimum: 1 piece.
